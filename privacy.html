Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Fa9Ssa DEV team has adopted this privacy policy to explain how we collect, store, and use information in connection with our Services. BY INSTALLING, USING, REGISTERING TO OR OTHERWISE ACCESSING THE SERVICES, YOU AGREE TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY. We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at reasonable times, so please review it frequently.

Non-Personal Data

For purposes of this Privacy Policy, "non-personal data" means information that does not directly identify you.

The types of non-personal data we may collect and use include, but are not limited to:

  • Application properties (name, package name, icon installed on your device)
  • Your checking (including likes, recommendations) of a game will be disclosed to all Fa9Ssa DEV users

Fa9Ssa DEV may use and disclose to our partners and contractors the collected non-personal data for purposes of analyzing usage of the Services, advertisement serving, managing and providing the Services, and to further develop the Services and other Fa9Ssa DEV services and products.

Note: Analytics companies utilized by Fa9Ssa DEV may combine the information collected with other information they have independently collected from other services or products relating to your activities. These companies collect and use information under their own privacy policies.

Personal Data

For purposes of this Privacy Policy, "personal data" means personally identifiable information that specifically identifies you as an individual.

Personal information collected by Fa9Ssa DEV is information voluntarily provided to us by you when you create your account or change your account information. The information includes:

  • Facebook ID, name, gender, location
  • Your friends' ID on Facebook
  • Game check-ins, likes, dislikes, recommendations and messages

Social Network Integration

Certain features of the Services may be able to connect to your social networking sites to obtain additional information about you. In such cases, Fa9Ssa DEV may be able to collect certain information from your social networking profile when your social networking site permits it, and when you consent to allow your social networking site to make that information available to Fa9Ssa DEV.

This information may include:

  • Name, profile picture, gender, user ID
  • Email address, country, language, time zone
  • Organizations and links on your profile page
  • Names and profile pictures of your social networking site "friends"
  • Other information you have included in your social networking site profile

Fa9Ssa DEV may associate and/or combine as well as use information collected by Fa9Ssa DEV and/or obtained through such social networking sites in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Disclosure and Transfer of Personal Data

Fa9Ssa DEV collects and processes personal data on a voluntary basis and it is not in the business of selling your personal data to third parties.

Personal data may, however, occasionally be disclosed in accordance with applicable legislation and this Privacy Policy. We may disclose personal data:

  • To our parent companies and subsidiaries
  • To agents and contractors who collect and process personal data on our behalf
  • To third party service providers (credit card processors, e-mail service providers, shipping agents, data analyzers)
  • As required by law enforcement or government officials
  • When necessary to protect Fa9Ssa DEV's rights, property, operations, or users
  • In connection with a corporate merger, consolidation, or sale of assets

Important: To the extent permitted by applicable law, Fa9Ssa DEV will make reasonable efforts to notify you of such disclosure through our website or in another reasonable manner.

Safeguards

Fa9Ssa DEV follows generally accepted industry standards and maintains reasonable safeguards to attempt to ensure the security, integrity and privacy of the information in our possession.

  • Only persons with a need to process your personal data have access to it
  • Personal data is stored in secure operating environments not available to the public
  • Personal data is maintained behind a firewall-protected server

Disclaimer: However, no system can be 100% secure and there is the possibility that despite Fa9Ssa DEV's reasonable efforts, there could be unauthorized access to your personal data. By using the Services, you assume this risk.
